Exploring the Cosmos, Shaping the Future

ISRO's pioneering research program is dedicated to unlocking the mysteries of space and advancing humanity's reach beyond Earth.

Discover Our Mission

Our Mission

To push the boundaries of space science and technology, fostering innovation and discovery for a sustainable interplanetary future. ISRO's research program aims to propel India into the forefront of cosmic exploration, from lunar missions to deep space probes.

Featured Projects

Chandrayaan-X

Next-generation lunar exploration, focused on resource mapping and sustainable habitation technologies.

Mars Odyssey

Advanced Martian orbiter and rover collaboration to study the Red Planet's climate and geology.

AstroSat 2.0

Multi-wavelength space observatory for studying black holes, exoplanets, and cosmic phenomena.
